Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

Kineticue is deeply committed to your privacy. Our core philosophy is local-first: all video processing, editing, and analysis are performed entirely on your local device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your personal videos or sensitive data to any external servers.

If you voluntarily contact our customer support, we may collect minimal information (such as your email address, device OS, and app version) solely for the purpose of resolving your inquiry.

2. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data remains yours.

3. Data Retention

Any information collected through customer support is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, after which it is securely deleted.
